This functionality is implemented for ES either on/prem or in the Cloud.

The 2 programs read the BUM csv logs on-prem and write them with enrichmentin ES either on -prem or in the Cloud

For writing in the Cloud, the prog needs parameters to connect to ES in the Cloud which are in the example.ini config file

- BUFF_Enrich_X_Cloud.py
- BUFF_Enrich_X.py
- example.ini
- param_data.json
